\Strategies

A creative tool inspired by Oblique Strategies. Generate thought-provoking prompts to help break through mental blocks in design, music, writing, and beyond.

Key Features

  • Shuffle and reveal curated Oblique Strategies-style prompts
  • Dark mode and distraction-free interface
  • Optional daily notification for a random strategy
  • Local-only app: no internet required

Privacy

This app collects no data, requires no login, and operates entirely offline. You can read the full Privacy Policy and User Privacy Choices below.

Privacy Policy

\Strategies does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not use analytics, tracking, or external services.

User Privacy Choices

As no personal data is collected, no privacy choices are necessary. All app activity remains fully local to the device.
