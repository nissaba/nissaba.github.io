EVE Structure Timer

Unofficial companion app for EVE Online capsuleers.

Privacy Policy User Privacy Choices

Privacy Policy

EVE Structure Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require user accounts, does not track users, and does not use any analytics tools. All data remains on the user's device and is not transmitted to any external server.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact the developer at: nissaba.dev@gmail.com

User Privacy Choices

EVE Structure Timer does not collect or track user data, and therefore there are no privacy choices or opt-out mechanisms required. Users are in full control of their data within the app, and no data is shared externally.
