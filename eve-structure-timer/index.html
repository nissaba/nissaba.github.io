EVE Structure Timer

A precision tool for EVE Online capsuleers to track structure vulnerability windows.

Key Features

  • Track shield, armor, and hull reinforcement timers
  • Display times in both EVE Time and local time zone
  • Receive alerts before key vulnerability windows
  • Organize timers by system or corporation
  • iCloud sync support

Privacy

This app does not collect or share any personal data. You can read the full Privacy Policy and User Privacy Choices sections below.

Privacy Policy

EVE Structure Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require user accounts, does not track users, and does not use any analytics tools. All data remains on the user's device and is not transmitted externally.

User Privacy Choices

As no data is collected, no user privacy settings or opt-outs are required. Users remain in full control of their data on-device.
