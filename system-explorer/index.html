EVE Frontier

System Explorer

A companion tool for exploring solar systems and their resources across EVE Frontier — planets, moons, asteroids, and more.

Key Features

  • Browse solar systems and view available resources
  • Inspect planets, moons, and asteroid belts at a glance
  • Search and filter systems to plan mining and exploration routes
  • Offline-friendly access to cached system data

Privacy

System Explorer does not sell your data or use third-party advertising trackers. Read the full Privacy Policy and User Privacy Choices below.

Privacy Policy

System Explorer is designed with privacy in mind. The app does not collect personal information beyond what is necessary to provide its features. Any data stored on your device stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to sync it through Apple's iCloud services.

The app may fetch public game data from EVE Frontier servers to display system and resource information. These requests do not include personally identifiable information.

If you contact us for support, we will only use your email address to respond to your inquiry and will not share it with third parties.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

User Privacy Choices

You can delete all locally stored data by removing the app from your device. If you use iCloud sync, you can disable it in your device Settings under Apple ID → iCloud → System Explorer.
